Sirloin steak with pickled mustard seeds and greens

Link to original recipe video by Adam Ragusea

Description

This is a summary of Adam Ragusea's recipe for sirloin steak with pickled mustard seeds and greens

Ingredients

  • Sirloin steak
  • Dark greens, kale for example
  • Mustard seeds
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Oil
  • Salt, pepper, sugar
  • Chili, shallot
  • Vinegar
  • Fish sauce or any alternative source of umami
  • Honey, optional
  • Chinese five spice, optional
  • Fresh cilantro, optional as garnish

Steps for the mustard seeds

  1. Boil about a half cup of whole mustard seeds then drain and rinse
  2. Repeat the draining and rinsing process to remove bitterness as desired
  3. Add vinegar, water, and/or sugar to the drained mustard seeds to taste
  4. Add pinches of salt to seeds to taste
  5. Fully submerge seeds and boil for 7-8 minutes, add diced vegetables such as chilis or shallots if desired
  6. Adjust with salt, sugar, or other flavors to taste

Steps for the sirloin and greens

  1. Prepare greens by cutting out stems and minorly chopping the leaves
  2. Peel and dice garlic and ginger prior to eating
  3. Cut between natural boundaries and trim out fat and unwanted tissue
  4. Season with salt, pepper, optional five spice, and rub with oil
  5. Cook the steaks on very high heat in a wide pan, cooking the sliced stems once the steaks are removed from the pan
  6. After 2 minutes, stir in the prepared garlic and ginger and add leaves from greens
  7. Cook down leaves and add fish sauce or prepared alternative
  8. Continue cooking greens for about 5 minutes and season with honey and other seasonings

Slice meat and plate with hot greens, spoon on mustard seeds and garnish with cilantro leaves if desired
