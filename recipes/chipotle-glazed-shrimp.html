Chipotle-glazed shrimp with garlic lime rice

Ingredients

Shrimp ingredients

  • 1 lb large, peeled shrimp
  • 0.5 can finely chopped chipotle peppers, adobo sauce
  • 0.5 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • salt

Rice ingredients

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 2.25 cups water
  • 4 peeled, chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 lime
  • 1 cilantro bunch
  • olive oil

Steps for the rice

  1. Add a bit of olive oil to a pot on medium heat
  2. Stir the dry rice and toast for a minute before adding in the garlic
  3. Add water, salt, and lime zest
  4. Bring the pot to a boil and cover with a lid, keeping the pot on a lower simmer
  5. Add cilantro and lime juice to taste once rice is finished cooking

Steps for the shrimp

  1. Create the glaze, combining honey, chopped chipotles, and tomato paste
  2. Optionally, add lime juice or vinegar into the glaze
  3. Skewer the peeled shrimp
  4. Place an oven rack on the highest possible position and turn the broiler on max
  5. Lay shrimp skewers on rack with a lipped baking sheet placed beneath
  6. Glaze the surface of the shrimp and sprinkle salt on top
  7. After a few minutes under the broiler, remove the rack from the oven, glaze the top side of the shrimp skewers again, and return the rack into the oven
  8. Repeat the previous step but flip the skewers to glaze the other side
  9. Glaze the second side once more for a total of four coats of glaze and broil "until you have some nice color"

Serve skewer with rice and optional lime wedge
